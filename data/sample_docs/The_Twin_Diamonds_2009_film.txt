The Twin Diamonds

Year: 2009
Director: Kon Khmer Koun Khmer collective (student directors)
Producer: Davy Chou
Genre: Suspense

Overview:
The Twin Diamonds is a 2009 Cambodian suspense film produced by Davy Chou and made by Kon Khmer Koun Khmer, a youth-driven filmmaking collective he helped found through film workshops with six universities and around 60 students. Directed by the student participants, it appeared during a wave of revived Cambodian filmmaking, much of which explored themes of family and infidelity.
