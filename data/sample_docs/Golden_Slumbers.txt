Golden Slumbers (French: Le Sommeil d'or)

Year: 2011
Director: Davy Chou
Genre: Documentary, Film History
Country: Cambodia, France

Overview:
Golden Slumbers examines the lost Golden Age of Cambodian cinema. Rather than centering surviving film prints, the documentary approaches that history through memory and testimony, preserving recollections of a once-thriving national film culture that was devastated during the Khmer Rouge era. It is an ode to Cambodian filmmaking and an important work about cultural memory, disappearance, and recovery.

Reception:
The documentary established French-Cambodian filmmaker Davy Chou internationally and screened at multiple film festivals. Chou later directed Diamond Island and Return to Seoul.
