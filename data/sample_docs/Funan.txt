Funan

Year: 2017
Director: Denis Do
Genre: Animation, Historical Drama
Country: Belgium, Cambodia, France, Luxembourg
Runtime: 84 minutes
Technique: 2D computer animation

Plot Summary:
During the Khmer Rouge period, a young Cambodian woman is separated from her son amid the forced evacuation of Phnom Penh. She must endure exile and the regime's violence while struggling to survive and find her child. The animated feature presents Cambodian history through a family's experience of separation, resilience, and survival.

Awards:
Funan received the Cristal for a Feature Film at the 2018 Annecy International Animation Film Festival.
