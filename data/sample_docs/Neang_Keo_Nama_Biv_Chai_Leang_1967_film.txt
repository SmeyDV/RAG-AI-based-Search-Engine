Neang Keo Nama (Biv Chai Leang version)

Year: 1967
Director: Biv Chai Leang
Genre: Legendary (boran / folklore legend film)

Cast:
·   Vann Vannak
·   Saom Vansodany

Overview:
Neang Keo Nama is a 1967 Cambodian legendary (boran) film directed by Biv Chai Leang, from the golden age of Khmer cinema. Two separate films of this title were produced in 1967, by different directors; this is the Biv Chai Leang version, starring Vann Vannak and Saom Vansodany. It was remade in 2005.
