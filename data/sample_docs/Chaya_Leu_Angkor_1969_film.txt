Chaya Leu Angkor

Year: 1969
Director: Norodom Sihanouk
Genre: Musical

Overview:
Chaya Leu Angkor is a 1969 Cambodian musical film directed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk, one of the nine features he made during the golden age of Khmer cinema.
