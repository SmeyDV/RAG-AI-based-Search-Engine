The Little Prince

Year: 1966
Director: Norodom Sihanouk
Genre: Golden-age Cambodian cinema

Cast:
·   Norodom Sihamoni

Overview:
The Little Prince is a 1966 Cambodian film directed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk. It is historically notable for starring Norodom Sihamoni - Sihanouk's son, who later became the King of Cambodia in 2004. One of the rare films of its era still in existence ("present existence").
