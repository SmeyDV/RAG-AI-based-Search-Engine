Chet Chong Cham (I Want To Remember)

Year: 1984
Director: Mao Ayuth
Genre: Drama

Cast:
·   Kai Prosith

Overview:
Chet Chong Cham (English: "I Want To Remember") is a 1984 Cambodian drama directed by Mao Ayuth (who later made the historical epic Loung Preah Sdach Korn), produced by Angkorwat Production. It dates from the revival of Cambodian cinema in the 1980s, after the fall of the Khmer Rouge had all but destroyed the film industry.
