Sovann Pancha

Year: 1970
Director: Yvon Hem
Genre: Legendary (boran / folklore legend film)

Cast:
·   Vann Vannak
·   Vichara Dany

Overview:
Sovann Pancha is a 1970 Cambodian legendary (boran) film directed by Yvon Hem, from the golden age of Khmer cinema. It is noted as actor Vann Vannak's only surviving film, and is one of the rare films of its era still in existence ("present existence"), most Cambodian films of the period having been lost during the Khmer Rouge era.
