Sappsitt

Year: 1965
Director: Ly Bun Yim
Genre: Legendary (boran / folklore legend film)

Cast:
·   Kong Som Eun
·   Virak Dara

Overview:
Sappsitt is a 1965 Cambodian legendary (boran) film directed by Ly Bun Yim, one of the most celebrated directors of the golden age, and starring Kong Som Eun and Virak Dara. It is one of only two films from 1965 still known to exist today ("present existence"), making it historically significant. It is one of the 13 films from 1965 that mark the beginning of the golden age of Khmer cinema. (No detailed plot summary has been published; this entry records only verified facts.)
