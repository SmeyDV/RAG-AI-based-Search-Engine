The Prey (2018 film)

Year: 2018
Director: Jimmy Henderson
Genre: Action

Cast:
·   Gu Shangwei as Xin
·   Vithaya Pansringarm as The Warden
·   Dy Sonita
·   Reth Tiger as Stunt Man

Plot Summary:
Xin, an undercover Interpol operative, investigates a phone-scam ring operating out of Phnom Penh that targets victims in China. When Cambodian police raid the operation's headquarters, Xin keeps his cover intact by letting himself be arrested alongside the criminals. The captured group is transported to a brutal, remote prison near the border, run by a sadistic warden who secretly sells prisoners to wealthy men who hunt them for sport in the surrounding jungle. Before he is released into the wilderness as human prey, Xin manages to signal his supervisor in Beijing. Set loose and hunted, he must survive the jungle, outfight the armed hunters and rival prisoners, and battle his way back to freedom.

Awards:
Premiered at the 2018 Busan International Film Festival.

Overview:
The Prey is a 2018 US-Cambodian action film directed and co-written by Jimmy Henderson, from the same team behind Jailbreak. Trumpeted as Cambodia's first million-dollar action movie, it showcases the traditional Cambodian martial art of bokator in its fight sequences. The film runs 87 minutes and was distributed internationally on Netflix.
