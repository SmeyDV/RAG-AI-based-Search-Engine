The Road to Freedom

Year: 2011
Director: Brendan Moriarty
Genre: War / drama

Cast:
·   Joshua Fredric Smith as Sean
·   Scott Maguire as Dana

Plot Summary:
In 1970, at the height of the Vietnam War, two adventure-seeking photojournalists, Sean and Dana, boldly sneak into war-torn Cambodia in search of evidence of Khmer Rouge atrocities. Braving the jungle to get their story, the pair are captured by Khmer Rouge guerrillas and disappear into the hands of enemy forces, where they struggle to regain their freedom.

Overview:
The Road to Freedom is a 2011 war film and the directorial debut of Brendan Moriarty, released in United States theaters and rated R. It is inspired by the real-life story of photojournalist Sean Flynn, son of Hollywood star Errol Flynn, who vanished with fellow photojournalist Dana Stone in Cambodia in 1970.
