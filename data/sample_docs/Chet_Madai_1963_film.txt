Chet Madai

Year: 1963
Genre: Drama

Overview:
Chet Madai is a 1963 Cambodian film, notable as the oldest surviving (still existing) Khmer film, rediscovered as of 2009. Most Cambodian films from this era were lost or destroyed during the Khmer Rouge period, making its survival historically significant.
