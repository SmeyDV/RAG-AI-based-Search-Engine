Tngai Chong Krouy (The Last Night)

Year: 1989
Director: Yuthara Chanee
Genre: Romance

Overview:
Tngai Chong Krouy (English: "The Last Night") is a 1989 Cambodian romance film, notable as the first film of director Yuthara Chanee, from the revival of Cambodian cinema in the 1980s after the fall of the Khmer Rouge.
