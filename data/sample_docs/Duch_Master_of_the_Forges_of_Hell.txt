Duch, Master of the Forges of Hell (French: Duch, le maître des forges de l'enfer)

Year: 2011
Director: Rithy Panh
Genre: Documentary, History
Country: Cambodia, France

Overview:
Rithy Panh's documentary centers on Kaing Guek Eav, known as Duch, who commanded the Khmer Rouge security prison S-21. Built around extended conversations with Duch, the film confronts the administration of imprisonment, torture, and mass murder. It continues Panh's long-running work of documenting the Cambodian genocide and preserving the memory of its victims.

Reception:
The film was presented as a Special Screening at the 2011 Cannes Film Festival.
