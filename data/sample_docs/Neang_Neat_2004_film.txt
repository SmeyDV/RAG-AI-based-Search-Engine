Neang Neat (Khmer: នាងនាថ)

Year: 2004
Genre: Horror

Plot Summary:
Based on a Khmer folktale said to have originated in Battambang province in the late 1800s, the film tells the story of Neang Neath, a beautiful woman who lives happily with her husband Doan in a small wooden house. One day, while returning home from selling necklaces, Doan is attacked by robbers and injured in the chest; at the same moment, Neath dies from severe complications while giving birth. After her death, Neath's ghost returns and kills the household maid. When Doan finally comes home, he sees the shadow of his wife holding their baby, and the two are reunited.

Overview:
Neang Neat (Khmer: នាងនាថ) is a 2004 Cambodian horror film based on the popular Khmer legend of Neang Neath, one of many Cambodian horror films rooted in traditional folklore and ghost stories.
