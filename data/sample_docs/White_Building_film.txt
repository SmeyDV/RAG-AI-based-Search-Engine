White Building

Year: 2021
Director: Kavich Neang
Genre: Drama
Country: Cambodia
Language: Khmer
Runtime: 90 minutes

Cast:
Piseth Chhun
Chinnaro Soem

Plot Summary:
The lives of three friends are upended when the artist enclave where they reside, the White Building, is scheduled to be demolished. The White Building was a real apartment complex in Phnom Penh that housed many artists and was a cultural landmark before its demolition in 2017. The film explores themes of displacement, gentrification, and the loss of community in rapidly developing Phnom Penh.

Reception:
White Building premiered at the 78th Venice International Film Festival on 7 September 2021. It was selected as the Cambodian entry for the Best International Feature Film at the 94th Academy Awards. The film was also nominated for the New Talent Award at the Hong Kong Asian Film Festival 2021.

Awards:
Cambodian submission for Best International Feature Film at the 94th Academy Awards (2022). Nominated for the New Talent Award at the Hong Kong Asian Film Festival 2021.

Overview:
White Building is a 2021 Cambodian drama film directed by Kavich Neang, starring Piseth Chhun. The film takes its name from the iconic White Building in Phnom Penh, a historic apartment complex that was home to many artists before its demolition. The film premiered at the Venice Film Festival and was Cambodia's official Oscar entry.
