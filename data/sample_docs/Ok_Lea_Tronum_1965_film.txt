Ok Lea Tronum

Year: 1965
Genre: Legendary (boran / folklore legend film)

Cast:
·   Mae Yasit
·   Dy Saveth

Overview:
Ok Lea Tronum is a 1965 Cambodian legendary (boran) film starring Mae Yasit and Dy Saveth. It is one of only two films from 1965 still known to exist today ("present existence"), making it historically significant, as most Khmer films of the era were lost during the Khmer Rouge period. It was remade in 2006. It is one of the 13 films from 1965 that mark the beginning of the golden age of Khmer cinema.
