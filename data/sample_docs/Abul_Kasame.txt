Abul Kasame

Year: 1968
Director: Yvon Hem and stars Kong Som Eun and Saom Vansodany

Cast:
Kong Som Eun
Saom Vansodany
Pech Saleoun
Mandoline
Chin Sinath

Overview:
Abul Kasame (Khmer: អាប៊ុលកាសេម, UNGEGN: Abŭl Kasém) is a 1968 Khmer film adapted from one of the many tales of 1001 Arabian Nights. The film is directed by Yvon Hem and stars Kong Som Eun and Saom Vansodany. The film has recently been discovered to be in existence in 2008. Another film similar to this, and is also directed by Yvon Hem, is Ynav Bosseba.
