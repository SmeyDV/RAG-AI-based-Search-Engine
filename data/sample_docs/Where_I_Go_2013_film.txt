Where I Go

Year: 2013
Director: Kavich Neang
Genre: Documentary / drama

Plot Summary:
The film follows San Pattica, an 18-year-old Cambodian of mixed parentage. His Cameroonian father came to Cambodia in the early 1990s as part of the United Nations Transitional Authority (UNTAC) and then left, never knowing he had fathered a child. Disowned by both his mother and grandmother and raised in a children's home, Pattica suffers daily discrimination. He confronts his family - and the UN - over their inability to provide him and his half-sisters with a decent home and education.

Overview:
Where I Go is a 2013 Cambodian documentary directed by Kavich Neang, who later directed the acclaimed feature White Building. It is an intimate portrait of a mixed-race young man born in the UNTAC era and the discrimination he faces.
