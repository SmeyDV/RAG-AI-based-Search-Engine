Ah Sach June Mdai (1989 film)

Year: 1989
Genre: Drama

Cast:
·   Ket Somborith
·   Kai Prasith
·   Oum Sovanny

Overview:
Ah Sach June Mdai is a 1989 Cambodian drama produced by Mokott Pich Production, from the revival of Cambodian cinema in the 1980s after the fall of the Khmer Rouge. (It shares its title with a 1968 golden-age legend film of the same name; this is the later, 1989 production.) (No detailed plot summary has been published; this entry records only verified facts.)
