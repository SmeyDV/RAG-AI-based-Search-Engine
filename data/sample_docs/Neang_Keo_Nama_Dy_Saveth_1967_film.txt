Neang Keo Nama (Dy Saveth version)

Year: 1967
Director: Dy Saveth
Genre: Legendary (boran / folklore legend film)

Cast:
·   Kong Som Eun
·   Dy Saveth

Overview:
Neang Keo Nama is a 1967 Cambodian legendary (boran) film directed by golden-age star Dy Saveth, who also stars alongside Kong Som Eun. Two separate films of this title were produced in 1967, by different directors; this is the Dy Saveth version. It was remade in 2005.
