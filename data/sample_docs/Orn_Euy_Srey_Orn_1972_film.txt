Orn Euy Srey Orn

Year: 1972
Director: Ly Bun Yim
Genre: Golden-age Cambodian cinema

Overview:
Orn Euy Srey Orn is a 1972 Cambodian film directed by Ly Bun Yim, one of the most celebrated directors of the golden age of Cambodian cinema, known for his pioneering use of special effects and for legend films such as 12 Sisters.
