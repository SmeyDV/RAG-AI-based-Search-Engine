Tears in a Quiet Purple Evening

Year: 1987
Director: Tep Rundaro
Genre: Drama

Overview:
Tears in a Quiet Purple Evening is a 1987 Cambodian film, the directorial debut of Tep Rundaro. It was made during the tentative revival of Cambodian cinema in the 1980s, after the fall of the Khmer Rouge had all but destroyed the country's film industry.
