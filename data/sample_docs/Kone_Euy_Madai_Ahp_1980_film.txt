Kone Euy Madai Ahp

Year: 1980
Genre: Horror

Overview:
Kone Euy Madai Ahp is a Cambodian horror film from the early 1980s (sources give 1980 or 1984) drawing on the Khmer folklore of the Ahp (also spelled Arb or Ap), a flying female spirit that is a staple monster of Cambodian ghost stories. It dates from the revival of Cambodian filmmaking after the Khmer Rouge era. (No detailed plot summary has been published; this entry records only verified facts.)
