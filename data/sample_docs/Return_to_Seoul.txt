Return to Seoul (French: Retour à Séoul)

Year: 2022
Director: Davy Chou
Genre: Drama
Country: France, Germany, Belgium, Cambodia

Plot Summary:
A young woman adopted and raised in France travels to South Korea, the country of her birth. Her unexpected visit develops into a search involving her biological parents and her own shifting sense of identity. The film follows her across several years as she negotiates adoption, family, belonging, and the distance between cultures.

Reception:
Return to Seoul was selected for Un Certain Regard at the 2022 Cannes Film Festival. It represented Cambodia in the international feature film competition for the 2023 Academy Awards.
