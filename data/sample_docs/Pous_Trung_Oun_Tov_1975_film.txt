Pous Trung Oun Tov

Year: 1975
Genre: Golden-age Cambodian cinema

Overview:
Pous Trung Oun Tov is a 1975 Cambodian film, noted as the last surviving Khmer film made before the Khmer Rouge seized power in 1975 and Cambodian cinema production collapsed. It marks the end of the golden age of Cambodian film. (No detailed plot summary has been published; this entry records only verified facts.)
