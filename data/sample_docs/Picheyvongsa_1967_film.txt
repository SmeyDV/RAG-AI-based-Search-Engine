Overview:
Picheyvongsa is a 1967 Cambodian legendary (boran) film directed by So Min Chiv, from the golden age of Khmer cinema. It proved enduringly popular and was remade twice, in 1996 and 2004. (No detailed plot summary has been published; this entry records only verified facts.)
