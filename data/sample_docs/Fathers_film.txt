Fathers (Khmer: ដើម្បីកូន)

Year: 2020
Director: Huy Yaleng
Genre: Drama
Country: Cambodia
Language: Khmer
Runtime: 110 minutes

Cast:
Chy Chhenhlin
Sonyta Mean
Kong Sophy
Huy Yaleng

Plot Summary:
Fathers tells the story of Cambodian family dynamics, exploring the relationships between parents and their children. The film focuses on the sacrifices and struggles fathers make for their families in contemporary Cambodia. The Khmer title, "Daeumbei Kon," literally translates to "For Children," reflecting the film's central theme of parental dedication and love.

Reception:
Fathers was selected as the Cambodian entry for the Best International Feature Film at the 93rd Academy Awards, representing Cambodia on the international stage at one of the most prestigious film award ceremonies. While the film was not nominated, its selection highlights the growing recognition of Cambodian cinema globally.

Awards:
Cambodian submission for Best International Feature Film at the 93rd Academy Awards (2021).

Overview:
Fathers is a 2020 Cambodian drama film directed by Huy Yaleng and starring Chy Chhenhlin, Sonyta Mean, Kong Sophy, and Huy Yaleng. It was selected as the Cambodian entry for the Academy Awards' Best International Feature Film category. The film explores themes of parental sacrifice and family bonds in modern Cambodian society.
