Cast:

Overview:
Neang Vimean Chan is a 1966 Cambodian legendary (boran) film directed by golden-age star Dy Saveth, who also stars alongside Kong Som Eun. It is one of the rare films of its era still in existence ("present existence"), though not yet released on DVD.
