Kamnat Het Neang Neath

Year: 2017
Genre: Horror

Overview:
Kamnat Het Neang Neath is a 2017 Cambodian horror film. Its title refers to Neang Neath, the subject of a well-known Khmer ghost legend that has been adapted to the screen more than once, and the film continues Cambodia's strong tradition of horror cinema rooted in folklore and ghost stories.
