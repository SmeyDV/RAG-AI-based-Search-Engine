Prey Prasith

Year: 1967
Director: Norodom Sihanouk
Genre: Legendary (boran / folklore legend film)

Cast:
·   Norodom Sihanouk

Overview:
Prey Prasith is a 1967 Cambodian film directed by (and starring) Prince Norodom Sihanouk, one of the nine feature films he made during the golden age of Khmer cinema. It is one of the rare films of its era still in existence ("present existence"). (No detailed plot summary has been published; this entry records only verified facts.)
